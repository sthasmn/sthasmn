The Ultimate Christmas Icebreaker

This is the complete guide for "Santa's Secret Ingredient," designed for our Christmas party of 100 guests. This document contains the game concept, rules, a visual flowchart, printable card designs, and the full list of recipes and ingredients needed for a successful event.

How to Play: Step-by-Step

1

Arrival & First Card

As each guest arrives, hand them one random **Ingredient Card**.

2

The Great Team-Up

To start the game, give each person a **Recipe Card**. Their first task is to find the 9 other people with the *exact same recipe*.

3

Inventory & Strategy

Teams gather to see what ingredients they have and what they need, then plan their trading strategy.

4

The Christmas Barter

The host announces trading can begin. Teams mingle and negotiate with other teams to get the ingredients they need.

5

Winning & Presentation

The first team with all their ingredients shouts "Merry Christmas!" To win, they present their recipe and creatively explain their **Secret Ingredient**.

Game Flowchart

Guest Arrives

Receives one random Ingredient Card.

Game Starts

Each guest receives a Recipe Card.

Team Formation

Guests find 9 others with the same recipe to form a team.

Strategy Phase

Team pools cards and plans who to trade with.

Trading Phase

Mass mingling and bartering between teams.

Recipe Complete!

First team with all ingredients shouts "Merry Christmas!"

Final Presentation

Winning team presents their dish and secret ingredient.

Prizes Awarded

Prizes for the winner and "Most Creative Presentation."
