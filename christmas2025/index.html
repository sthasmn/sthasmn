The Ultimate Christmas Icebreaker

Welcome to the official guide for "Santa's Secret Ingredient"! This game is designed specifically for our Christmas party, with a diverse group of 100 guests including Rotarians, scholarship students, and alumni. The goal is to create maximum fun, interaction, and laughter with simple, easy-to-understand rules.

Game Concept

A collaborative trading and storytelling game. Guests form teams to "bake" a magical Christmas recipe. The catch? The necessary ingredients are scattered among all 100 guests. Teams must mingle, negotiate, and trade cards to complete their recipe and win.

Objective

To be the first team to collect all the ingredients for your recipe and creatively present your finished "dish" to everyone.

How to Play: Step-by-Step

1

Arrival & First Card

As each guest arrives, hand them one random **Ingredient Card**. This is their starting item for trading.

2

The Great Team-Up

To start the game, give each person a **Recipe Card**. Their first task is to find the 9 other people with the *exact same recipe*. This naturally forms 10 teams of 10 and gets everyone talking immediately.

3

Inventory & Strategy

Once teams are formed, they gather to see what ingredients they have and what they need. This is where they plan their trading strategy.

4

The Christmas Barter

The host announces, "Let the trading begin!" Teams must now mingle with other teams to trade for the ingredients they're missing. This is the main part of the game and generates the most energy.

5

Winning & Presentation

The first team to collect all their ingredients runs to the stage and shouts "Merry Christmas!" To win, they must present their recipe and creatively explain the importance of their **Secret Ingredient**.

Materials Checklist (For 100 guests)

  • 10 Unique Recipe Concepts: Create 10 different whimsical Christmas recipes.
  • 100 Recipe Cards: 10 copies of each of the 10 unique recipes.
  • ~100 Ingredient Cards: Individual cards for each ingredient listed on the recipe cards. Ensure you have exactly one of each "Secret Ingredient" and duplicates of more common ones.
  • Prizes: One main prize for the winning team and an optional prize for the "Most Creative Presentation."
